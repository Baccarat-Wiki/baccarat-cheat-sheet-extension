Basics of Baccarat

Baccarat is an exciting game where you predict which hand Player, Banker, or Tie will total closest to 9.

Step 1 of 5: Place Your Bets

Choose one of three options:

You bet that the Player’s hand will have a total closer to 9 than the Banker’s hand.

Payout: 1:1
House Edge: ~1.24%

You bet that the Banker’s hand will win. This is statistically the safest bet in Baccarat.

Payout: 1:1 (5% fee)
House Edge: ~1.06%
